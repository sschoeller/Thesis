\subsection{Mappings}
Mapping $f$ with domain $X$ and range $Y$ is a subset of $X x Y$ s.t. for each element $x \in X$, precisely one element $y \in Y$ for which $(x, y) \in f$.
$f: X \to Y$\\
The image of $f$ is: $fA = \{ y \in Y | y = f(x), x \in A \}$
